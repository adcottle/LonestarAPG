Forum Login Options

To login to the LSC Forum you must enter your user name and password EXACTLY as shown in your "Welcome" e-mail message. You can also turn on the "Always stay logged in" option or you will be automatically logged out after 60 minutes (or whatever value you type into the "Minutes to stay logged in" box. New members will receive a "Welcome" e-mail message once they are added to the LSC Forum by the administrator. Existing members should have received a "Welcome" message on 28 March 2007 when the new Forum was started.

THE FORUM HOME WINDOW




Each time you login to the LSC Forum the Forum Home window will be displayed.

The HEADER section of most LSC forum pages includes:
  • Web site URL, logo, and forum name
  • a HELLO message with your user name and the date/time you logged in
  • QUICK LINKS to
    • "Show unread posts since last visit" (THIS IS THE QUICKEST WAY TO SEE WHAT HAS CHANGED SINCE YOUR LAST VISIT if you don't use e-mail notifications. This doesn't work on your first login as you have no "official" last visit time.)
    • "Show new replies to your posts"
  • Total time logged in - this sometimes doesn't appear to be accurate - it can be ignored
  • SEARCH box and button - to search forum messages - this search button does NOT search the online help
  • News line (may be blank, can contain news items from the administrator)
  • TOP NAVBAR (more on navigation below)
The BOARDS section includes a title line and one entry per board:

Member Forum Title Line (If you click on the icon next to "Member Forum" it closes the list of boards and changes the icon to a +. Just click again to reopen the board list.)

BOARDS

Column 1 = new post indicator
  • Dark Blue icon indicates the board has messages you haven't read
  • Lighter icon indicates the board has no new messages

Column 2 = board name
  • ANNOUNCEMENTS BOARD - for Chapter announcements
  • ACCESS OTHER MEMBERS-ONLY PAGES BOARDS - contains links to members pages that are not part of the forum itself - such as meeting registration pages, merchandise order forms, etc.
  • CHAPTER MENTORING PROJECT BOARD - for Mentoring Project information
  • GENERAL DISCUSSION BOARD - the primary board for communication - topics that are not specific to the other boards
Column 3 = number of posts and topics in the board

Column 4 = information on the last post made to the board


INFO CENTER - a summary of posts and online members with links to additional statistics on the forum usage - this section is primarily useful to forum administrators

THE BOARD WINDOW

Click on a BOARD Title in the FORUM HOME window to view the TOPICS on that board.

The BOARD page includes:
  • the TOPICS within this board
    • Column 1 = Topic "hotness" indicator (see ICON KEY)
    • Column 2 = Message type indicator (text, PDF, etc.)
    • Column 3 = TOPIC name - click to read the topic post and replies
    • Column 4 = Author name
    • Column 5 and 6 = number of replies and times topic was viewed/read
    • Column 7 = Most recent post to this topic
  • BOARD ACTIONS navbar
    • MARK READ - mark all of these topics as read
    • NOTIFY - THIS IS THE OPTION THAT MAKES THE FORUM WORK LIKE A MAIL LIST.

      After setting e-mail notifications preferences you also click one or more of the NOTIFY buttons. Then when a new TOPIC or reply is posted in this BOARD you will receive e-mail just like on a mail list. Do this for EACH of the boards and everything on the forum will come to your e-mail inbox.

      If you do not want to be notified of ALL POSTS to the BOARD do not use this button. Each individual TOPIC screen also has a NOTIFY button to allow you to be notified of replies to the individual TOPIC.
    • NEW TOPIC - click here to start a new topic on this BOARD (more on this below)
  • a "bread crumb trail" shows your location within the forum - you are in the General Discussion Board of the Member Forum at lonestarapg.com.
  • an ICON KEY and a JUMP BAR to let you quickly navigate to another board without going back to the FORUM HOME page

CREATE A NEW TOPIC OR REPLY TO A TOPIC

Click on the NEW TOPIC button in the BOARD window to start a new topic. A similar screen is displayed when you click REPLY to an existing topic.

The forum should be used to discuss any topics of interest to Lone Star Chapter APG members. This is our place to freely discuss topics of interest to many of us. Personal messages between members should be handled via direct e-mail or using the "Private Message Feature" of the forum.

To create a new topic:
  • enter a subject (this will be prefilled if you REPLY to an existing topic)
  • and enter your text into the large text box.
  • After entering your message
    • click "Spell Check" and correct any spelling errors,
    • then click "Preview" to see what the message will look like when displayed,
    • then click "Post" to send the message or reply.
  • All other items are OPTIONAL and can be ignored by most users but are briefly described here:
    • most of the time the standard message icon (looks like 2 pieces of paper) will not need to be changed but there is an option to change it to
      - thumbs up or thumbs down
      - exclamation point or question mark
      - lamp or smiley with several smiley options
    • the buttons can be used to make text bold, italics, etc. Placing your mouse cursor over a button will display a tooltip telling what the button will do to the text. These work similar to the buttons in a word processor in that you enter text, highlight the text then click a button to apply that option to the highlighted text. Instead of displaying in bold, etc. what you will see are some codes inserted to cause the text to be displayed in your selected format. If you aren't familiar with HTML coding this may look strange but you can use the preview button under the message text box to be sure the message will display as you desired.

      The buttons are: bold    italics   underline   strikethrough   glow   shadow   marque   preformat-text   left-align   center   right-align.
    • Other buttons will be familiar to those who know HTML or design Web pages and can be ignored by those who don't.

      The buttons are: hr   font-size   font-face   change-color-box   flash   image   hyperlink   e-mail   ftp-link   table   table-row   table-column   superscript   subscript   TTY code   quote   list.
    • A row of smileys allows you to click on one and the code for it will be inserted into your message at the point where the cursor is sitting

FIRST TIME LOGIN

Each new member of Lone Star Chapter APG is added to the forum by the moderator. The FORUM WELCOME message will contain your user name and password. To login to the forum you must enter your user name and password EXACTLY as shown with the same punctuation and capitalization. If an error has been made in your user name contact our webmaster and we will change it. The welcome e-mail will be similar to: 
       Subject: Welcome to lonestarapg.com

       Hi XXXXX XXXXX,

       You are now registered with the Lone Star Chapter APG 
       member forum at lonestarapg.com.

       Your account's username is XXXXX XXXXX and its password is XXXXX.

       You may change your password after you login by going to your 
       profile, or by visiting this page after you login:

       http://lonestarapg.com/forum/index.php?action=profile

       Regards,
       The lonestarapg.com Team.
You should immediately use the link in the e-mail to login using the password supplied in the e-mail.

This will take you to the profile page for your login. On the left-hand side of the page is a navigation bar (navbar). Under the "Modify profile" section click on "Account Related Settings." You should change your password immediately and configure the basic user preferences.

On this page you can also change your e-mail address, choose a new password, and enter a secret question to be used when you need a reminder for a forgotten password. (Please note that "Enter favorite pet" is not a very secure "Secret question" and is included here ONLY to show you can enter any desired secret question prompt. Use a question only you would know the answer to.)

YOU MUST HAVE YOUR CURRENT PASSWORD TO MAKE ANY CHANGES ON THIS PAGE - enter it into the "Current Password" box. After entering data in the appropriate boxes and your current password click on the "Change profile" button to apply the changes.

BASIC PREFERENCE CONFIGURATION

To configure other profile preferences click on "Forum Profile Information" in the left-hand "Modify Profile" navbar.

Any of these preferences can be left blank if you prefer not to share the information:
  • Personal text - will be displayed under your member name on all of your posts and replies
  • Birthdate - enter any or all of month, day, and year
  • Location - enter state or city, state, etc.
  • Gender - use drop-down menu to select gender
  • Instant Message numbers - ICQ AIM MSN YIM (leave these blank if you don't do instant messaging)
  • Signature - will be displayed at the bottom of each post or personal message you send on the forum.
  • Website title
  • Website URL
Click on "Change profile" after all changes are entered. Any of this information can be changed by accessing this same page in the future. See the TOPIC WINDOW section to see how these items are displayed in topics you post.

NOTIFICATIONS AND EMAIL

To configure basic notification preferences click on "Notifications and Email" in the left-hand "Modify Profile" navbar.

Setting the options as shown results in the forum simulating a mail list except you still must login to the forum to post a reply to a message. If you prefer other options use the online HELP button in the TOP NAVBAR to learn how to set the options.

After setting these options you must go to each BOARD and click the NOTIFY button to get e-mail notifications of all posts on that BOARD. You also have the option of setting NOTIFY on individual TOPICS within a board. These buttons are explained above in the BOARD WINDOW and TOPIC WINDOW sections.

After making changes click "Save settings" to put those changes into effect.

"Current Topic Notifications" and "Current Board Notifications" sections will indicate the topics and boards where you have requested notification.

More advanced options are available in the "Look and Layout Preferences" and the "Personal Message Options" sections. Users interested in changing these advanced options can probably determine how to set them using the online HELP.

FORGOTTEN PASSWORD

If you have forgotten your password you can change it via an e-mail link or use a "Secret question" to prompt you for a term that will allow you to change your password in the browser.

To change your password click on "Forgot your password?" in the login window.


Enter your user name exactly as shown in your initial welcome e-mail with same capitalization and punctuation. Then click the "Send" button.


You will then see the message shown below:


A short time later you will receive a message at your member e-mail address similar to the one below. X's below will be replaced by information specific to your member login. 
    Dear XXXXX,

    This mail was sent because the 'forgot password' function has been 
    applied to your account. To set a new password click the following link:

    http://lonestarapg.com/forum/index.php?XXXXXXX

    IP: XX.XX.XX.XX
   
    Username: XXXXX XXXXX

    The lonestarapg.com Team.


Click on the link in the e-mail message. When the window below is displayed enter a new password in both the "Choose password" and "Verify password" boxes then click the "Save" button.



If the password is successfully set the window below will be displayed and you can click the "Login" button to login to the forum.

ALTERNATE PASSWORD CHANGE METHOD

If you don't have access to e-mail or just prefer to use an alternate method you can use the "Ask me my question" method. After clicking on "Forgot your password?" turn on the "Ask me my question" option before clicking the "Send" button.


If you correctly enter the answer to your secret question (as set in your profile during your initial preference configuration) you can then enter a new password in both the "Choose password" and "Verify password" boxes and then login to the forum.

CHANGING E-MAIL ADDRESSES OR PASSWORDS

To change your e-mail address or current password in the forum:
  1. Login to the forum.
  2. Click on the "Profile" button in the top navigation bar (navbar).

    This will take you to the profile page for your login. On the left-hand side of the page is a navigation bar (navbar). Under the "Modify profile" section click on "Account Related Settings."
  3. Type your new e-mail address into the "Email" box AND/OR type a new password into the "Choose password" and "Verify password" boxes.
  4. Type your current password into the "Current password" box.
  5. Click the "Change Profile" button.
  6. Notify the Chapter Membership Coordinator of the e-mail address change. In the future you should be able to change your e-mail address in the member database yourself but for now the coordinator must do so.
